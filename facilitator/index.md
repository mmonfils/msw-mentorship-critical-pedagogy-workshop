## Session Deliverables & Facilitation Guides

### Kickoff & Logistics (5 Minutes)
* **Goal**: Welcome attendees, review the schedule, display the QR code, and explain the embedded Google Form entry method.
* **Ethics Connection**: Frame active participation as part of social work professional development and lifelong learning (NASW Code of Ethics 3.02).

---

### Block 1: Institutional Guidance & Faculty Accessibility
* **Briefing (15 Minutes)**:
  * Distinguish between administrative logistics (class schedules, paperwork) and developmental role modeling.
  * Discuss how rapid communication (under 24 hours) reduces anxiety and builds self-efficacy.
  * Connect developmental support to CSWE Competency 1 (Demonstrate Ethical and Professional Behavior).
* **Breakout (7 Minutes)**:
  * Prompt attendees to complete Section 1 of the form on their mobile device.
  * Focus question: How can faculty transition routine office hours away from procedural questions toward career visioning and praxis?

---

### Block 2: The Non-Traditional & Remote Student Experience
* **Briefing (15 Minutes)**:
  * Address systemic barriers facing adult learners, working parents, and remote students.
  * Examine the low margin of error for non-traditional students and the phenomenon of acting as their own "blockers."
  * Connect tailored support to CSWE Competency 2 (Advance Human Rights and Social, Economic, and Environmental Justice).
* **Breakout (7 Minutes)**:
  * Prompt attendees to complete Section 2 of the form.
  * Focus question: What specific administrative obstacles can we automate or eliminate so non-traditional students don't have to act as their own blockers?

---

### Block 3: Near-Peer Support & Horizontal Networks
* **Briefing (15 Minutes)**:
  * Detail horizontal and nested mentoring models (advanced students mentoring incoming cohorts).
  * Highlight how mutual learning distributes mentoring responsibilities while developing leadership capacity.
  * Discuss reciprocal benefits for both mentors and mentees.
* **Breakout (7 Minutes)**:
  * Prompt attendees to complete Section 3 of the form.
  * Focus question: How can we structure near-peer mentoring so advanced students receive academic credit or leadership development without adding unpaid emotional labor?

---

### Block 4: Insights & Live Q&A (19 Minutes)
* **Real-Time Data Synthesis**: Switch screen display using the [Live Form Summary Link](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1oTGbdRKRUA71BIIhExpmrs8NRxs6L36-lD9TDrkZaAo/edit#responses) to showcase live participant submissions across Blocks 1, 2, and 3.
* **Discussion**: Lead an interactive Q&A synthesizing audience insights into concrete policy recommendations for academic and field settings.
